                             Letter of  Walter Phillips, dean of
                                    Rochester ,  1560  
                         

                         
                             Epistola  Gualteri Philippi  decani
                                Roffensis ad universam pubem ludi literarii  Roffensis  
                         

                         
                             in chartis 
                         

                         On a leaf of paper stuck in after f.  212v , written in
                            cent.  xvi  
                         Uniuerse pubi  Roffensis 
                            ludi literarii  Gualterus Philippus  p. D.
                            S. 
                         Quoniam in huius noui anni auspicio 
                         Thanking them for a letter and wishing them good progress 
                         
                             Denique hoc unum restat a vobis exorandum, et spero confore,
                                videlicet ut vos qui nullas adhuc ad me dedistis literas sicuti
                                iussum erat a preceptore, velitis saltem ad petitionem meam sibi
                                dare libenter treis plagas, et sic dando (ni fallar) aliquid
                                accipietis, ut hunc nouum annum felicius auspicaremini 
                         
                         Dated  Rochester 
                             13 January 1560 . 3 Elizabeth
