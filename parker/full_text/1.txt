                          Gregory the Great ,
                                 Miscellaneous extracts  
                         A further collection out of  Gregory 's works of expositions on Scripture, the extracts
                            being arranged in the order of the Biblical books. The headline is
                                 Tabula Sumpta  
                         Begins imperfectly in c. xv (on Genesis) 
                         Each book is preceded by capitula 
                         Ends imperfectly on Haggai. The New Testament books are treated before
                            the Prophets
