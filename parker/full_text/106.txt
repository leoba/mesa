                             Of the excommunication of  Dr. Cliff 
                                 1529  
                         
                         
                              Dr. Cliff  was chancellor to the  bishop of Ely , and having excommunicated a
                                scholar, he was himself excommunicated by the vice-chancellor as
                                guilty of perjury in infringing the privileges of the university;
                                the doctor refusing to submit to this sentence, the cause was
                                referred to  cardinal Wolsey , who
                                determined it in favor of the university 
                         
                          Lamb,  Original
                                    Documents  (1838) , 12
