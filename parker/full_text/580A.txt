                             Parker Certificates ( Bedford ) (Missing
                                leaves from CCCC MSS  97  and
                                     122 ) 
                         
                         So-called Parker Certificates: tabulated copies of the returns from part
                            of the diocese of Lincoln ( Bedfordshire ).
                            These returns give details of each incumbent and were sent in answer to
                                 Parker 's requests of  1560 
                            and  1561  (see  J. Bruce and T. T. Perowne,  Correspondence of Matthew Parker , 1853, xci
                                and cxi ).
