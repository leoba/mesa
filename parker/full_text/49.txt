                             Three scribes seem to be distinguishable. One writes the
                                Pentateuch (?), Psalms, Maccabees etc. Another is seen in Proverbs
                                etc. The third in the Prophets and Epistles. On the whole I believe
                                the work to be French 
                         
                         In the book (at Ecclus. XXXV) is a contemporary book-marker, consisting
                            of a disc of vellum which revolves between two other pieces so shaped
                            that only half of the disc is exposed. It is inscribed in this wise:
