                 Apparently a  Winchester  book,
                    from a Continental archetype of  Cologne diocese . 

                  The first nine quires are in a beautiful round hand: the rest in an
                        equally good hand but less remarkably beautiful . 

                  On the flyleaf is pasted a full-page woodcut of
                        the Crucifixion, on vellum, from a Missal (as in  MS 162 ) . 

                 On the verso is a  Parkerian  list of contents. 

                 The book is not in  W. H.
                        Frere's list of English Pontificals (Alcuin Club Collections
                    1900) .
