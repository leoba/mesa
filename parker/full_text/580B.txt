                             Parker Certificates ( Ely ) (Missing
                                leaves from CCCC MSS  97  and
                                     122 ) 
                         
                         So-called Parker Certificates: original return from the  diocese of Ely . These returns give details of
                            each incumbent and were sent in answer to  Parker 's requests of  1560  and  1561 
                            (see  J. Bruce and T. T.
                                Perowne,  Correspondence of Matthew Parker ,
                                1853, xci and cxi ). 

                     
                     
                         Letter from  Richard Cox, bishop of
                                Ely , to  Matthew Parker ,  24
                                January 1560/1
