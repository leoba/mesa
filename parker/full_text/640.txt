                             Antiphoner leaf 
                         

                         Musical notation for antiphon, verse and responsory.
                            Begins imperfectly in antiphon for Magnificat at First Vespers for the
                            Seventh Sunday after Pentecost: (Unxerunt  Salomonem 
                             Sadoc sacerdos  et  Nathan  pro)pheta regem in Gihon: et procedentes laeti
                            dixerunt uiuat rex  Salomon  seculorum. This
                            differs slightly from the wording of the antiphon in the 1568 editio
                            princeps of the  Breviarium Romanum  

                         Historia  Salomonis  Ad
                            Vesperam 

                         
                             3-line calligraphic ornamental initial tinted in pale yellow
                                for the versicle: Dilectio illius custodi 
                         
                         Ad Matutinum Resp. XII 
                         
                             3-line calligraphic ornamental initial tinted in pale yellow
                                for the responsory: Audi fili mi disciplinam Patris
