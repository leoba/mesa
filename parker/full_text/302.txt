                 T. James' entry is 275  Homiliae 52. Saxonice  which is
                    incorrect so far as this book is concerned: but no other identification seems
                    possible.  K.-A. Rand has identified, following R. Page's note, that M.
                        R. James has confused T. James 272 for 275. The 52 A-S. Homilies (T. James
                        275) are in  MS 162   


                 This is MS. E in  Skeat's
                             Aelfric's Lives of Saints  (see II, p.xiv) .
                    He uses it for the Homilies on Ash Wednesday and on Auguries.  Napier  uses it for no. XLIX in his
