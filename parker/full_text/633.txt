                             Greek Gospel Lectionary (fragments) 
                         


                         ff.  1r - 6v  daily lessons from John, from
                            (part of) Sunday to Saturday of the last week before Pentecost 
                         ff.  7r - 10v  Saturday and Sunday lessons
                            from Matthew as far as 4th Sunday after Pentecost 
                         f.  11r  and  11v  parts of same sequence for
                            7th Saturday and Sunday and 8th Saturday 
                         f.  12r  and  12v  parts of same sequence for
                            11th Saturday and Sunday 
                         f.  13r  and  13v  parts of same sequence for
                            16th Sunday and 17th Saturday, ending with cross-references to lessons
                            from Matthew for 17th Saturday and John for 17th Sunday 
                         f.  14r  and  14v  parts of lessons for
                            Thursday in Holy Week 
                         f.  15r  and  15v  part of first of 12 Gospels
                            for Good Friday 
                         f.  16r  and  16v  parts of 4th and 5th
                            Gospels for Good Friday
