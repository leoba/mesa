                             First Epistle of John in Latin and Arabic 
                         

                         The Latin begins 
                         In nomine Patris, etc. Dei unius Amen.   Epistola
                            Juchannae filii Zebdei, prima: quae est in ordine (Catholicarum)
                            quarta.   Cap. I.   1. Praedicamus vobis illum (corr. to in illo)
                            qui perstitit (non amotus est) ab initio, etc. 
                         There are a good many corrections of the Latin, which occupies nine
                            leaves 
                         The Arabic occupies five leaves and is  very well written, with
                                some attempt at ornamentation, by a native scribe
