                             Plutarch ,  Quaestiones Graecae et
                                Romanae (excerpted from Moralia, book 4) 
                         

                         The text of ff.  1r - 8v  has been erased more
                            or less completely 
                         The volume contains a Latin version of the Quaestiones Romanae and
                            Quaestiones Graecae of  Plutarch  
                         On the early Latin versions see  Fabricius,  Bibl. Gr.  V  203 
                         The first complete section is on f.  9r  
                         Quid est quod ancillis eadem Matute ingredi fas non
                            est 
                         (no. 16) 
                         The Roman questions end  59v  
                         et cognatorum quod bruto accidit 
                         The Greek continue with no separate title 
                         Qui sunt quos epidauri compodes artinos
                            appellant 
                         A good many blanks are left in the text as if the archetype were
                            defective. The text of ff.  81r - 82v  is
                            erased beginning in the question (no. 46) 
                         Quid est quod purgatorem uocant thralian
                            (!) 
                         The first after the blank is (no. 52) 
                         Quid cause quamobrem elei equas finibus eductas
                            iniri faciunt 
                         Ends 
                         postea a genere hamasocii liste (άμαξοκυλιστὰι)
                            nominati sunt 
                         Finis
