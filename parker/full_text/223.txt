                 This MS. is mentioned by  Traube,  Nomina
                        Sacra  , p. 232, as being one of those in which the contraction nrt

                            ix to xi  at  Corbie ,  St Bertin ,  Reims ,  Troyes ,  Langres ,  Fleury  and  Tours .  It is also
                    noticed by  Dümmler in  Neues Archiv  IV 533 .
