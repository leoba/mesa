                     considerans  or  uocat  

                 Three flyleaves at the beginning are from a
                         xiii th cent. MS. of decretals  in small
                        hand . Then a blank leaf ( i ). 

                 f.  iir  a has
                         xvi th cent. note and title. 
                 On  iiv 
                     a drawing of the seven-branched candlestick with allegorical
                        explanation, and diagram of 'transitus filiorum israel per mare
                        rubrum'.  
                 ff.  iiir - viiir  have the compendium of sacred history up to the time of
                    Christ which is often found written on rolls and attributed to  Petrus Pictavensis :  
                     Considerans hystorie sacre prolixitatem    with genealogical
                    tables. 
                 On  viiiv 
                     a drawing of a seraph, with the allegorical significance of the six
                        wings and feathers . 
                  There are some very handsome
                        decorative initials containing grotesque figures, e.g. Genesis f.
                             1v ; Exodus (a centaur)  f.
                             19r  ; Numbers (retaining its cloth guard)
                             f.  34v  ; Joshua, curious figure
                             f.  44r   .
