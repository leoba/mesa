                             Initials are rough, filled with patches of dull red and

                             The first obvious change of hand is on f.
                                 13v  
                         
                          A third hand, much larger and rounder, appears on ff.
                                     37r - 38v  , thus 
                         Liber I, Capitulum xxxviii. De observatione dierum
                            etc.

                         et sunt omnes dies tamquam dominica

                         Paulisper de ministratoribus persecutionis Christi
                            quid actum sit uideamus

                            ebraicorum nominum

                            uendidit
