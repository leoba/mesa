                 The added leaves are supplements from another manuscript.
                    They comprise  the twelve chapters which are left out of the  Antwerp  edition of  1587  . Also in
                    this MS. the chapters in Liber I  which in all editions are numbered 14 and 15
                        are found after cap. 17 .   The above is from Mr Howlett's notice of
                    the MS. ( Chronicles of Stephen etc. Rolls
                        Series I p. xlviii ). He also says that this copy is to be classed with
                         Bodleian Digby 101  and  Royal 13. B. ix .
