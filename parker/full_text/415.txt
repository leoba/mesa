                 This volume contains the famous series of tracts by the
                         Yorker Anonymus : described by  Hampe,  Neues Archiv  XXII  669. (He
                    distinguishes 7 or 8 scribes.) The greater part of the text has been printed, as
                    the references given below will show, in  MGH. ( Libelli de Lite )  or  H. Böhmer,  Staat und Kirche ,
                        1899 .
