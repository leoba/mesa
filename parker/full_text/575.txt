                 Formerly catalogued as printed book SP. 13. 

                 Rough sheep binding, eighteenth century, blind-tooled. 

                 A list of the books and manuscripts bequeathed by  Matthew
                        Parker  to  Corpus Christi College,
                    Cambridge ; copies of two indentures laying down conditions for the care
                    of books and plate bequeathed by Parker; details of some books given after
                    Parker's time, etc. Copies of the Register were given to the other two colleges
                    named in the indenture:  Gonville and Caius
                        College (MS 710/743)  and  Trinity Hall
                        (MS 29)
