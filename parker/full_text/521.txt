                             De signis pestilencie 
                         
                         De signis pestilencie 
                         Hic videndum est de signis pestilencie. pro quo
                            sciendum quod de numero signorum pestilenciam signancia 
                         There are 10 signs:   Conjugation of bad planets.   Comets,
                            etc.   Long westerly or southerly winds, cloudy weather especially
                            from middle of May to July.   Cloudy and wet summer.   Variation
                            of temperature.   Hot days and cold nights.   Numbers of frogs,
                            toads, mice, fleas.   Animals come up from their holes in the
                            ground.   Birds leave their nests.   Weather is unseasonable
